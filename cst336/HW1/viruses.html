What Is a Computer Virus?

A computer virus is a malicious program that attaches itself to files or software, spreading between systems and networks. Some may only cause minor disruptions, while others can delete data or disable entire systems.

Types of Viruses and Malware

  • Worms: Self-replicating malware that spreads automatically.
  • Trojans: Disguised programs designed to steal or damage data.
  • Spyware: Software that secretly monitors user activity.
  • Ransomware: Encrypts data and demands payment for release.
  • Adware: Displays unwanted ads and collects browsing data.

Prevention Tips

  • Keep antivirus software up to date.
  • Be cautious with attachments and links.
  • Enable automatic system updates.
  • Use strong passwords and MFA.
  • Backup data regularly.
