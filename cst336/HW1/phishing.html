What Is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of social engineering attack where criminals impersonate legitimate organizations through emails, calls, or text messages to trick users into giving away personal or financial information.

Types of Phishing

  • Email Phishing: Fraudulent messages pretending to be from trusted sources.
  • Vishing: Voice calls designed to steal sensitive data.
  • Smishing: Text messages with malicious links or attachments.

Recognizing Phishing Attempts

  • Unusual sender email or generic greeting.
  • Urgent requests or warnings.
  • Spelling errors or poor formatting.
  • Suspicious links or attachments.
